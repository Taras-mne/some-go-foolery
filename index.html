How it works

Three steps to your personal cloud

No technical knowledge required. If you can install an app and pick a folder, you're done.

01
Install the daemon

Download the installer for your OS. Run it. The daemon starts automatically on boot and runs silently in the background.

claudy-setup.exe → Next → Finish
02
Pick a folder

Choose any folder on your machine. Claudy wraps it in a WebDAV server and connects it through a secure tunnel to your account.

/Users/you/MyCloud ✓
03
Mount on any device

Use the WebDAV address from your dashboard. iOS, Android, Windows, macOS and Linux all mount it natively — no extra apps needed.

dav.claudy.app/you
Features

Built for real use, not demos

Everything that matters. Nothing you don't need.

Your data stays yours

Files live on your machine, not our servers. The relay only proxies encrypted traffic — we never see your content.

No VPS, no ports

Works behind NAT, CGNAT, and firewalls. Our relay punches through without exposing your IP or opening ports.

Native on every OS

WebDAV is built into iOS, Android, Windows, macOS, and Linux. No client apps to install on the devices you access from.

Runs in the background

Install once, forget it exists. The daemon autostart on boot and keeps your folder online without any maintenance.

Device management

See every device connected to your cloud. Remove access instantly from your dashboard — like Spotify, but for your files.

P2P in v2

Direct device-to-laptop connections via WireGuard when network allows. Relay stays as fallback. Faster transfers, less latency.
